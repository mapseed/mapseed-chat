Seattle CouncilChat is a simple way to discuss the meetings of the Seattle City Council as they happen.

Read & follow the Code of Conduct for this project. In short: act like a jerk and you'll get removed. Don't be a jerk.

Interested in projects like this?

You should get involved with Code for Seattle, a local organization focused on improving civic engagement with technology.

You should also join the seattle.io newsletter to get updates on Seattle CouncilChat and similar projects.
